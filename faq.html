Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about plastic recycling and our services

What types of plastic does EcoCycle Solutions recycle?

+

We recycle a wide range of plastics, including PET (1), HDPE (2), PVC (3), LDPE (4), PP (5), and PS (6). Our advanced sorting technology allows us to process multiple types of plastic waste efficiently.

Why is plastic recycling important?

+

Plastic recycling is crucial for several reasons: it reduces waste in landfills, conserves natural resources, reduces energy consumption compared to producing new plastic, and helps prevent plastic pollution in our oceans and environment.

What happens to the plastic after it's collected?

+

After collection, plastics are transported to our processing facilities where they are sorted by type, cleaned, shredded, and processed into flakes or pellets. These recycled materials are then sold to manufacturers who use them to create new products.

How can I start recycling with EcoCycle Solutions?

+

You can start recycling with us by contacting our team through the contact form on our website, calling our customer service number, or sending us an email. We'll discuss your specific needs and set up a recycling program that works for you.

What services does EcoCycle Solutions offer?

+

We offer a comprehensive range of services including plastic collection, processing, corporate recycling programs, and community recycling initiatives. Each service is customizable to meet the specific needs of businesses, communities, and organizations.

Do you provide recycling bins?

+

Yes, we provide custom recycling bins for our collection services. The size and type of bins depend on your specific needs and the volume of plastic waste you generate.

How often do you collect plastic waste?

+

Collection frequency depends on your specific needs and the volume of waste generated. We offer weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly collection schedules, as well as on-demand pickups for larger quantities.

Can you help develop a recycling program for my company?

+

Absolutely! Our corporate recycling programs are tailored to the specific needs of each business. We provide program development, employee education, waste audits, and ongoing support to ensure successful implementation.

What is your recycling process?

+

Our recycling process involves several steps: collection, sorting, cleaning, shredding, processing, and transformation into new raw materials. We use advanced technology to ensure efficient and effective recycling.

Do I need to clean plastics before recycling?

+

While it's helpful to rinse containers, we have advanced cleaning systems in our facilities. However, removing food residue and emptying containers improves the quality of recycled materials and prevents contamination.

How do you ensure the quality of recycled materials?

+

We employ rigorous quality control measures throughout our process, including optical sorting technology, contamination removal systems, and final product testing to ensure our recycled materials meet industry standards.

What happens to non-recyclable materials?

+

Non-recyclable materials are separated during the sorting process. We work to minimize waste by finding alternative uses for these materials when possible, such as energy recovery, or ensuring proper disposal in accordance with environmental regulations.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept various payment methods including mobile money, bank transfers, credit cards, and electronic payments. We work with our clients to establish a convenient payment method that suits their needs.

How does mobile money payment work?

+

Our mobile money payment system allows you to pay for services using your mobile phone. Simply select the mobile money option, enter your phone number and the service you're interested in, and follow the prompts on your phone to complete the payment.

What are your pricing structures?

+

Our pricing varies depending on the service, volume of materials, collection frequency, and specific requirements. We provide customized quotes based on your needs. Contact our team for a detailed price estimate.

Do you offer discounts for long-term contracts?

+

Yes, we offer competitive pricing for long-term contracts. We work with our clients to develop cost-effective recycling solutions that provide value while meeting their sustainability goals.
