Our Services

Comprehensive plastic recycling solutions for a sustainable future

Plastic Collection

We provide convenient plastic collection services for businesses and communities. Our team ensures that plastic waste is properly collected, sorted, and transported to our recycling facilities.

  • Scheduled pickups
  • Custom collection bins
  • On-site waste audits
  • Data tracking and reporting

Plastic Processing

Our state-of-the-art processing facilities transform plastic waste into high-quality recycled materials. We use advanced sorting, washing, and processing technologies to ensure maximum recovery and quality.

  • Multi-stream processing capabilities
  • Advanced contamination removal
  • Quality control at every stage
  • Various output formats (flakes, pellets, etc.)

Corporate Recycling Programs

We partner with businesses to develop and implement comprehensive recycling programs. Our tailored solutions help corporations reduce waste, meet sustainability goals, and improve their environmental footprint.

  • Custom program development
  • Employee education and training
  • Waste reduction strategies
  • Sustainability reporting

Community Recycling Initiatives

We work with communities, schools, and local governments to implement effective recycling programs. Our community initiatives focus on education, accessibility, and long-term sustainability.

  • Community collection events
  • Educational workshops
  • School recycling programs
  • Public awareness campaigns
