About Us

Leading the way in sustainable plastic recycling solutions

Our Story

Plastic solution enterprises was started in 2020 in kampala uganda , co-founded by Nuwahereza Patricia ,Panuha Morris, Dawaru Maurine, Nalugya Desire .The world is facing a plastic crisis where millions of plastic wastes are generated .The greater kampala areas generate aprocymently 135,804 tones of plastic waste each year. This has led to the growing need of effective plastic waste management solution and promoting a cleaner and greener environment.

Plastic solution enterprises was founded to address this critical issue. Our team of experts has extensive experience in waste management, recycling and sustainability. We recognize an opportunity to make a positive impact by providing a reliable efficient plastic recycling services.

Our Mission

To transform plastic waste into valuable resources through innovative recycling solutions, while promoting sustainability and environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

A world where plastic waste is minimized, resources are conserved, and communities thrive in a cleaner, more sustainable environment.
