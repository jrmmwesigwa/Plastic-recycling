About Us

Leading the way in sustainable plastic recycling solutions

Our Story

EcoCycle Solutions was founded in 2008 with a simple mission: to transform plastic waste into valuable resources. What began as a small local recycling operation has grown into a leading innovator in the plastic recycling industry.

Over the years, we've expanded our operations, refined our processes, and developed cutting-edge technologies to maximize the recovery and quality of recycled plastics. Today, we serve businesses, communities, and organizations across the country, helping them implement effective recycling programs and reduce their environmental footprint.

Our Mission

To transform plastic waste into valuable resources through innovative recycling solutions, while promoting sustainability and environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

A world where plastic waste is minimized, resources are conserved, and communities thrive in a cleaner, more sustainable environment.

Our Values
